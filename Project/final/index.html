About This Project

When I was young, it was usually up to me to cook for myself after getting back from my part time job in the afternoon while my parents were working. While this wasn’t what gave me my love for cooking, it did instill it as a valuable skill for me. It was only as I got older that I really grow to appreciate it as a skill I wanted to improve on; however, by that point I had moved out and didn’t have much to work with. By the time I really wanted to get better at cooking, I had downgraded from a full kitchen, to only an electric burner and a convection oven, and without the luxury of picking out whatever ingredients I wanted from the store. But I still loved cooking, so I worked with what I had, and I realized that no matter the budget, it is always possible to make great food. And I would like to share some of that here.
