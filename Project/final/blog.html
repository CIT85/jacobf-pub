Last night I made butter chicken. I didn’t have heavy cream, so I used half&half. There’s probably a grandmother out there who would slap me for that, but I think I got it creamy enough by adding cream cheese and thickening it with a cornstarch slurry. I didn’t have every spice the recipes recommend, but I improvised a bit and I really think the flavor worked out! Unfortunately, I did overcook the rice, and while that did fill me with an immeasurable despair, it was all still good as a whole.
